Kalanchoe Thyrsiflora


Water: Every two to three weeks.


Since this is a succulent it will not need too much water. It is best to check the soil to see if it is still moist or if it still dry. Best lighting for it is light shade or direct sun, but you should be careful of direct sun lighting during hot days.
