White Trillium


Water: Only if soil is too dry.


White Trillium flowers are outdoor flowers. They prefer to be in rich soil gardens rather than in containers. They are best in partly shaded areas with not that much direct sun. White Trilliums do not need water as they will be outside your garden, as long as the soil is relatively moist then it is fine.
