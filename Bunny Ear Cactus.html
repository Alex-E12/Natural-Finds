Bunny Ear Cactus


Water: Every 3-4 weeks.


Should contain partial sun during the winter and full light growing for most of the year. A new Bunny Ear Cactus should be given water once every 1-2 weeks to create strong roots. After a while, should only water once every 3-4 weeks.
