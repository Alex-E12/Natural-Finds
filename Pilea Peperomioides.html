Pilea Peperomioides


Water: Weekly or every took weeks.


Pilea Peperomioides or Chinese Money Plants are mainly indoor plants benefits air purification and are set to bring good luck. Pilea Peperomioides also need a good amount of sunlight. Depending on the climate you should check the soil and if it is still moist/wet then do not water it after one week.
