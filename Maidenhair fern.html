Maidenhair fern


Water: In between every day and every other day.


A bright space without any direct sun is perfect for this flower. This flower is very demanding when it comes to watering. Should try to keep soil moist at all times to prevent it from dying. Watering would usually occur everday or every other day to prevent it from drying out.
