Blue Orchid


Water: Every 3-4 days.


Requires high humidity levels during the summer season. Needs around 6-8 hours of sunlight everyday. Should be watered every 3-4 days. Make sure to soak the roots for at least one minute after watering and drain any excess water. Watering and humidity should be reduced off growing season.
