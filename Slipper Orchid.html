Slipper Orchid


Water: Every 3-7 days.


Requires a good balance between shade and sunlight with reduced brightness. It is essential to water in the morning, as it allows water to evaporate throughout the foliage for the duration of the day. Should be watered every 3-7 days when its soil is nearly dry.
