Aerangis Orchid


Water: Soak 15 minutes once a week or every other week


High amounts of sunlight is required, but the sunlight must be indirect as they prefer shade over direct light. They should be kept in an incredibly moist environment, they must be well watered at all times, but not too much to drown it.
