Goat's Horn Cactus


Water: Once every 1-2 weeks.


Grows really well in full sun and can also grow under partial shade. The Cacti grow really well under room temperature but can also tolerate cold temperatures. If the Cacti begins to shrivel then that's a sign that it needs more water. Should water in the morning every 1-2 weeks.
