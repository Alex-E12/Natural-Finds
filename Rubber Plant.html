Rubber Plant (Ficus Elastica)


Water: In between every 1 to 2 weeks


Rubber Plants could be outdoor plants, but they are good home/indoor plants. They sun so putting them near a window where natural lighting reaches is always a good idea, although direct sun is too much for them. Rubber Plants should be watered every 1 or even 2 weeks, but if the soil is still moist then it can be watered every 3 weeks depending on the climate.
