Dahlia


Water: 2-3 a week.


Thrive under 6-8 hours of direct sunlight. Avoid any unnecessary shade as this will affect their bloom. Dahlia's thrive at normal room temperatures and will not tolerate the cold. Must water 2-3 times a week and even more often during hot climates.
