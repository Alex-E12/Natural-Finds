Echeveria Peacockii


Water: Every 1-2 weeks in dry season, 2-3 humid season.


It needs a high amount of light to thrive. The more it is given, the better it will fair and grow. Should however be given some protection from the harsh rays. Requires little water and is sensitive to overwatering.
