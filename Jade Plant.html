Jade Plant


Water: About every 2-3 weeks depending on season.


A few hours of sunlight per day should be enough lighting for this plant. They thrive in natural humid conditions. For watering, allow the soil to become dry before each watering. This could occur anywhere in between 2-3 weeks and changes depending on each season. Avoid overwatering the bottom as this can cause the roots to rot.
