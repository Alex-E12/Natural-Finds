Peruvian Zinnia


Water: Every 5-7 days.


Best grown under full sun for atleast 6 hours, but might need some shade in hot climates. The Peruvian Zinna can tolerate some drought rather than overwatering. Typically needs 1 inch of water every 5-7 days.
