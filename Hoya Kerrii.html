Hoya Kerrii


Water: Every two to three weeks


They require lots of light to grow and thrive indoors. Choose a location for your Hoya Kerrii that receives several hours of bright, direct sunlight every day. In the summer you can water more frequently while in the winter you should cut back on watering. Your Hoya Kerrii needs a lot of sunlight, if it isn't getting enough then consider getting a full-spectrum LED grow light.
