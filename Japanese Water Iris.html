Japanese Water Iris


Water: 1-2 Inches every week.


Requires a minimum of 4-6 hours of direct sunlight everyday. Avoid unnecessary shade to prevent it from not blooming. These Iris need about 1-2 inches of water every week. They perform best in moist soil. After establishing it is not necessary to water Iris.
