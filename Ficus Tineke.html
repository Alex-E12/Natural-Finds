Ficus Tineke


Water: Every one to two weeks.


likes the type of bright, indirect light that mimics that found in its natural tropical forest habitat. These plants appreciate it if you let the top couple of inches of the potting mix dry out to the touch in between waterings. So watering once every one to two weeks during the growing season is usually enough.
