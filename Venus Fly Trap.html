Venus Fly Trap


Water: once every 2-4 days.


Requires a minimum of 4 hours of direct sunlight a day and 8 more hours of diffused lighting. Venus Fly Traps thrive under humid conditions and should be watered through the bottom of their bases. Always used distilled water for watering to prevent any damages in its root.
