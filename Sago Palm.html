Sago Palm


Water: once every 1-2 weeks.


Warning! Poisonous to cats and dogs. Should be provided with regular sunlight hours, without directly hitting it. Water when soil is slightly dry at the top. Avoid watering the crowns as it can rot the plant. The plant should be watered every 1-2 weeks.
