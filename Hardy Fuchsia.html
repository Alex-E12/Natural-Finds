Hardy Fuchsia


Water: Once every 2-3 days.


Mostly grown in full sun or partial shade, but appreciates mostly shade on a sunny summer day. Grows best in a humid environment. Should be watered every 2-3 days. If the plants are wilt, it means they need more watering. If they are looking droopy, then you will need to water them more.
