Zygopetalum Orchid


Water: Once every 7-9 days.


It needs bright, filtered light to bloom. Providing some type of partial shade. Frequent blooming of these orchids can be encouraged with more frequent watering, as often as every two days throughout the growing season. after blooms drop then watering can be reduced to once a week. This plant is also sensitive to salts and minerals in tap water, so it's best to use filtered or distilled water at room temperature.
