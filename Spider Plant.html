Spider Plant


Water: Every two weeks


Spider Plants are good indoor and outdoor plants. Spider plants help clean indoor air and absorb toxic chemicals such as carbon dioxide and xylene. They must be kept at a moderate temperature and lighting. Water once a week is the recommended amount, but if the soil is still very moist then holding back the water for a couple of days should be fine.
