False Shamrock


Water: Every 1-2 a week during humid season, every 4 weeks during dry season.


Some direct sunlight with a mixture shade is ideal for this plant. Always water from the bottom to prevent rotting the bulbs. Start watering when the top inch of the soil is dry to the touch. Make sure that atleast 5cm of the soil is moist after watering. Watering could be as frequent as every 1-2 weeks. Should only water every 4 weeks during winter time.
