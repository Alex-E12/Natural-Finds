Pachira


Water: Weekly


Once the Plant soil becomes dry, remove excess water from the pot. The Pachira growth will slow down during the winter so it won't be needed to water too much. Avoid over watering as the pot. will become waterlogged. Pachira should be watered 3 tables spoons of water every week.
