Queens Tears


Water: once every 1-2 weeks.


These plants are very easy to raise as they bloom without assistance frequently. Should provide partial shade with bright but indirect light. Should avoid direct summer sunlight as it can destroy flowers. Water the soil and any flower cups pointing upwards. The rest of the flower should be sprayed with bits of water to keep it moist. You can do this every 1-2 weeks.
