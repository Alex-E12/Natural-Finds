Beach Spider Lily


Water: once every 9 days.


Demands partial shade and partial sun. The Beach Spider Lily requires the same amount of watering until growth emerges from the bulb and continues watering during the growing season. After leaves wither away in the winter, leave soil dry. Should water to keep the soil moist but never soggy. Usually requires 0.8 cups of water every 9 days.
